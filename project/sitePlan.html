The site purpose is to display information about my country of Honduras and let people know a little bit about
its culture and beautiful places to visit there. There will be a place to enter information if they would like to
travel to one of those places

What is the main language spoken in Honduras and what are some dialects? What is the best time of the year to visit Honduras?
