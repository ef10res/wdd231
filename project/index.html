What language is spoken in Honduras and what are some dialects?

Before Spain conquered Honduras, the main spoken language was Lenca. Now the main language is Spanish but there are dialects still spoken like Ch'orti', Garifuna, Miskito, Pech, Tol, and Tawahka.

What is the best time of the year to visit Honduras?

The best time is during the dry season, November to April. There is minimal rainfall and outdoor activities will be best during this season.
